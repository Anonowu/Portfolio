Overview

About the project

Bloom is a direct-to-consumer skincare brand launching a new digital channel. I designed the complete shopping experience — from brand discovery through checkout — built for their core audience of 25–40 year-old urban women.


Their existing Shopify template had no UX customisation. The challenge was to elevate the experience to match their premium brand positioning while driving measurable conversion improvements.

The Problem

What was broken

Bloom's existing store was a Shopify template with no UX customisation. Conversion rate was 1.2% — well below the 3.4% industry average for premium skincare.


Cart abandonment sat at 78%. The brand's premium positioning was completely lost in the experience — the store felt generic and untrustworthy.

  • No product guidance for skin type matching
  • Generic Shopify template — no brand identity
  • 78% cart abandonment rate
  • No social proof or trust signals
  • 7-step checkout causing drop-off

Outcomes & Impact

The results

The Skin Quiz guided users to products suited to their skin type, reducing choice paralysis. Editorial PDPs with skin-type-filtered reviews built trust. The new checkout reduced steps from 7 to 3.

Bloom launched to their best-ever quarter. The experience became their primary acquisition driver and a key differentiator versus competitors.

  • Conversion rate rose from 1.2% to 3.4% — matching category benchmark
  • Average order value grew 3.2× through bundle recommendations
  • Cart abandonment fell from 78% to 41%
  • App Store rating: 4.8 stars at launch
  • Skin Quiz completion rate: 68% of new visitors

Reflections

What I learned

🎯
Guidance beats choice
Users don't want more options — they want the right option surfaced for them. The Skin Quiz reduced choice paralysis and massively improved satisfaction and conversion.
🤝
Trust is a design surface
Every element of a premium checkout is a trust signal. Security badges, clear returns policies, and real reviews aren't just nice-to-haves — they're conversion levers.
📦
Personalisation compounds
Personalised bundle recommendations based on quiz data tripled AOV. Once you have user context, use it everywhere — consistently.
